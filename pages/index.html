A more perfect union

Please take your phone off of silent and turn your sound all the way up.

Use the toggle switch to start playing a melody. Everyone's phone will be playing a different melody.

If you don’t like your melody, turn the toggle switch off and back on again. You will then hear a new melody.

The amount of time you listen to a melody affects whether aspects of that melody are generated on another person’s phone. The longer you listen, the greater the chances.

You control the outcome of the installation. Through choosing sounds you like and skipping ones you don’t, we will create a work of collective sound art.

From the chaos will emerge A more perfect union.
